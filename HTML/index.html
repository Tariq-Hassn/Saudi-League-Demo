Al Hilal

Al Hilal Saudi Football Club is a Saudi sports, cultural, and social club that was founded in 1957, headquartered in the Saudi capital, Riyadh, and is considered the first team in Saudi Arabia in terms of the number of local championships.

Al Nassr

Al-Nasr Football Club is a Saudi football team that was founded in 1955 AD corresponding to 1375 AH. The team is nicknamed by its fans “The World” because of its participation and representation of the Asian continent in the first FIFA Club World Cup after winning the Asian Super Cup title in 1998.

Al Shabab

The Saudi Sports and Social Cultural Youth Club or the Saudi Youth Club “abbreviated” is a Saudi cultural and social sports club located in the city of Riyadh, and it is the oldest club in the capital, Riyadh. Al Shabab Club was established in 1947.

Al Ittihad

Al-Ittihad Club of Saudi Arabia, often referred to by the acronym Al-Ittihad. It is a professional football club, founded in 1927, in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. The club has spent its entire history in the top division since the establishment of the Saudi League.
